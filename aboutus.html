About Us


Welcome to the flavorful world of BBC Korean Fried Chicken, where every bite tells a story of culinary delight! Nestled in the heart of Bacoor, our local gem is a haven for those craving the tantalizing taste of Korean-inspired goodness.

At BBC, we're not just about chicken; we're about crafting an unforgettable experience. Picture yourself diving into a crispy, succulent piece of chicken, bursting with the rich flavors of Texas BBQ, the tangy zest of Orange Chicken, or the irresistible allure of Yangnyeom sauce. Each bite is a journey to savory paradise, leaving you craving more with every bite.





But wait, there's more! Our menu isn't just limited to mouthwatering chicken. We pride ourselves on offering a diverse selection of add-ons and sides that perfectly complement our star dish. From crispy fries to fresh salads, there's something for everyone to savor.

But beyond the food, BBC is a place where friends gather, laughter echoes, and memories are made. Our warm ambiance and friendly staff ensure that every visit feels like coming home.

So, whether you're a devoted fan of Korean cuisine or a curious foodie looking for your next culinary adventure, come join us at BBC Korean Fried Chicken. Prepare to tantalize your taste buds and embark on a flavor-filled journey that you won't soon forget!
